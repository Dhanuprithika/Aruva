# Aruva Project Report

## Project Overview

Aruva is an AI-powered agricultural marketplace and decision-support platform designed to connect farmers, buyers, and retailers. The platform aims to improve market accessibility, price transparency, and agricultural decision-making through market intelligence, multilingual accessibility, and AI-driven recommendations.

---

## Problem Statement

Farmers often face challenges in finding reliable buyers, obtaining fair market prices, and accessing real-time market information. Buyers and retailers struggle to discover trustworthy suppliers and evaluate market trends efficiently.

Aruva aims to bridge this gap by providing a digital platform that facilitates direct trade and data-driven agricultural decisions.

---

## User Roles

### Farmer

* Create and manage crop listings
* View market insights
* Manage buyer interest requests

### Buyer

* Search and discover crop listings
* Send interest requests
* Explore suppliers

### Retailer

* Discover suppliers
* Source crops in bulk
* Access market intelligence

## Key Decisions

* Single account = Single role
* Role selected during registration
* Separate dashboards for each role
* Admin dashboard postponed to future releases
* Documentation-first development approach
