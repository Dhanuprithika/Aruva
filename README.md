# Aruva

An AI-powered agricultural marketplace and decision-support platform that connects farmers, buyers, and retailers through market intelligence, multilingual accessibility, and intelligent recommendations.

---

## Features

### Farmer Module

* Crop listing and inventory management
* Marketplace participation
* Market price insights
* Interest request management

### Buyer Module

* Crop discovery and search
* Advanced filtering and supplier exploration
* Interest requests to farmers

### Retailer Module

* Bulk crop sourcing
* Supplier discovery
* Market intelligence access

### Market Intelligence

* Live market price tracking
* Historical price analysis
* Market trend insights

### AI Agents

* Market Intelligence Agent
* Crop Recommendation Agent

### Accessibility

* Multilingual support (English and Tamil)
